We want you to know who your politician is

As citizens of the United States, you have the right to representation in government. But lately, politicians seem to take that right for granted, saying one thing and voting a different way.

VoterAware wants to give some power back to the people. We track politicians and maintain lists of their voting habits, giving you access to that information.

DISCLAIMER

Steps to how the computer analysis works:

  1. All bills are designated as for or against a specific issue (Once a bill is designated for or against an issue, we have no input)
  2. The politician's voting record is then placed in the computer formula
  3. Politicians' are then given percentages for their tendencies to vote for or against each individual issue. Politicians are encouraged by VoterAware to provide written reasoning for their stances and voting records.
